A Clear Process

This process we use for guiding clients through a brand strategy & identity design process.

01

Discovery - Project Starts

We meet to talk through your business goals, customers and what you would like the new brand to accomplish. In this session, we will ask focused questions designed to help you think about your business from a new perspective. This Information will form the basis for our market research.

Information we need from you

Our meeting will give us everything we need, but you can share documents that help us understand your business better like a business plan or strategy document after our meeting if you like.

02

Research and style scapes and wireframes

Based on the discovery session, we will look into competitors, businesses with the same customers and general trends in design that apply well to your business. We will use this Information to put together a research report and style scapes (focused mood boards) to show opportunities for your brand. We also create wireframes to show the suggested structure of the website and the content on each page.

Information we need from you

Feedback during the meeting

03

Concept meeting

Knowing the direction from the research and style scapes, we create multiple concepts for your new brand and website. You select the direction you think is best for the company and let us know If you would like any Iterations before we move forward.

Information we need from you

Feedback during the meeting

04

Iterations and hand-off

Once the concept is clear, we will make any Iterations you have asked for and put together the brand guide and finished website design. You will also get access to all files you need day to day like templates or fonts.
